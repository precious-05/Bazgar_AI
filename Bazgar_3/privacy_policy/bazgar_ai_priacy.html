Privacy Policy

بزگر سنگت اے آئی - Bazgar Sangat AI

Last Updated: June 2025

Information Collection

Bazgar Sangat AI does not collect any personal information from its users. The application does not require account creation, login, or any form of user registration.

Camera Access

The application requests camera access solely for the purpose of capturing images of apple leaves or fruits for disease detection. This access is:

  • Used only when you explicitly use the camera feature
  • Never used for any other purpose
  • Not stored on any server after processing
  • Not shared with any third party

Data Storage

Images uploaded for disease detection are processed in real-time by the AI model and are not permanently stored on any server. The only data stored is:

  • RL (Reinforcement Learning) agent memory stored locally in your browser's LocalStorage
  • This data never leaves your device
  • You can clear this data at any time using the "Reset Memory" button in the Crop Assistant
Important: All image processing happens locally through your browser and our backend API. No images are saved, archived, or used for training without your explicit consent.

Analytics & Tracking

Bazgar Sangat AI does not use any analytics tools, tracking cookies, or third-party tracking services. We do not monitor user behavior or collect usage statistics.

Newsletter Signup

If you choose to sign up for our newsletter, your email address is collected but:

  • Is only used to send you agricultural information
  • Is never sold or shared with third parties
  • You can unsubscribe at any time

Third-Party Services

Bazgar Sangat AI does not share any user data with third-party services. All processing is done internally through our own backend infrastructure.

Legal Compliance

We comply with applicable privacy laws and regulations. Since we collect no personal data, there is minimal risk to user privacy.

Children's Privacy

Bazgar Sangat AI is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.
